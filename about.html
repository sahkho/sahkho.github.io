About

Hi there!

I'm a Junior at Cornell studying Information Science. I previously studied business, but then realized banking wasn't the move.

I got into product design since I was interested in behavioral economics and art, and the combinations of both fields led me here.

I've previously worked at Facebook, Intuit, Function of Beauty (YC '16), startups around Dallas, and freelancing. I also did some non-profit work with Fair Trade USA to help determine the true price of coffee and the Smithsonian Zoo on their compost education, manufacturing, and compost products.

In school, I lead the design team for CUAppDev and teach a class called Introduction to Digital Product Design (INFO 1998).

Currently, I'm building a podcast & video series called Students Who Design to help more students with un-conventional backgrounds break into the industry.

Please feel free to get in touch with me if you want to chat or work together!
